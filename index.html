Shape of Wars

Shape of Wars is a visualization based on p5.js. It is a collection of wars from 1823 to 2003, during this period of time, countless wars has happended.
Here I'm not trying to show you data about numbers, I just want to present the shape of wars based on a timeline.

Data Source: The Correlates of War Project
